The experiments include:
* Doppler-free saturated absorption spectroscopy of rubidium
* Spatial light modulation of laser light
* [[Scattering of Light from Particles|Mie, Rayleigh, and Tyndall scattering of light]]
* Single-photon interference
* Surface plasmon resonance of thin-films and fluids or Raman scattering microscopy(?)
* Temperature stabilization of a helium neon laser
* [[Acoustic Trapping of Particles|Acoustic trapping of particles in fluids]]
* Taylor-Couette fluid flow
* Digital image correlation of fluid flow
* Chaotic systems
